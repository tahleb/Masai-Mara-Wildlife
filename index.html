About Me

Hello! I'm Tahleb,a passionate wildlife photographer born in the picturesque town of Narok and raised amidst the breathtaking landscape of Maasai Mara. Growing up surrounded by the untamed beauty of nature and the majestic wildlife, I developed a deep appreciation for the natural world.

My journey into the photography began as a way to capture and share the awe-inspiring moments I witnessed daily. From the graceful stride of lioness to the vibrant hues of a sunset over the wild and it's wonders.

Through my lens, I strive to bring the beauty of wildlife to life, capturing the essence of each creature and the environment they inhabit. My work is not just about taking pictures ; it's about preserving the magic of nature and inspiring others to appreciate and protect our planet's incredible biodiversity.

what am i doing?

  • My portfolio spans various genres, including wildlife, landscape, and cultural photography. Each photograph is a testament to my dedication to preserving the essence of the Maasai Mara and beyond. Through my lens, I strive to bring the viewer closer to the heart of Kenya, highlighting its unparalleled beauty and diversity.
